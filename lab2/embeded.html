Sports Week

Sports Week at ACHS was a vibrant celebration of athleticism, teamwork, and college spirit. Students enthusiastically participated in a variety of sports events that tested endurance, strategy, and skill. The campus buzzed with excitement as teams competed with dedication and sportsmanship.The event not only promoted physical fitness but also encouraged unity among students. From track and field events to indoor games, Sports Week provided a platform for students to showcase their talents and build lasting memories.
